10th ke baad kya kare?

Career options & best guidance

10th class ke baad students ke paas kai options hote hain, lekin sahi decision lena sabse mushkil hota hai. Is article me hum aapko simple language me best options batayenge.

Science Stream

Agar aap Maths aur Science me interest rakhte ho, to Science stream best option ho sakta hai. Isme Engineering, Medical jaise career options milte hain.

Commerce Stream

Commerce un students ke liye accha option hai jo accounts, business aur finance me interest rakhte hain.

Arts Stream

Arts stream me History, Geography, Political Science jaise subjects hote hain. Ye UPSC aur government exams ke liye strong base deta hai.
